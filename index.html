Welcome to the Electric Vehicle Team

Design

Our design team works on getting sponsorships, making graphics, creating shirts and other promotional material and keep our club looking sharp.

Build

The focus of the team is on one of our three electric vehicles: A 2005 Kawasaki Ninja ZX6RR rebuilt into an electric superbike.

Race

We tune and improve our bike to perform in races such as the eMotoRacing all-electric race series, represnting RIT and racing against other schools.
